Bamboo Regional Hub


2020.03
We are currently developing a buiness model around the idea of a Bamboo Regional Hub. The Hub will consist of 3 components working in synergy:

  1: Bamboo Nursery;
  2: Bamboo Treatment Facility;
  3: Craft Workshop

The basic idea is to nurture an inclusive economy around the ecology of bamboo in its natural life cycle.
Due to the cyclical nature of the bamboo growing and harvesting, cultivating and processing bamboo utilizes different operational cycles throughout the year.

Production

1. The Bamboo Nursery will facilitate the reproduction of desirable bamboo species throughout the year or whenever possible.
Several propagation methods will be explored. We would like to establish a stable bamboo seedling supply chain for farmers and growers.

2. In order to turn raw bamboo into a usable construction material, chemical treatment and curing is necessary.
Untreated the bamboo will only last 2-3 years compared to 30-40 years treated and used undercover.
The Bamboo Treatment facility will facilitate various treatment techniques, including: Boucherie method (primary method), treatment pools (hot and cold).
The treatment facility will also be equipped for curing and drying of bamboo poles and strips.

3. This is a space which enables the processing of bamboo into various products.
Among these include daily utensils; furniture and housing components such as wall partitions; roof trusses; doors; screens etc.
As we grow as a business, we hope to invest in processing technology to enable us to produce standardized products.
Skills development training programs for bamboo and earth construction will also take place at the Craft Workshop.

Challenges

1. Access to Funding / Investment Capital
2. Mapping existing bamboo
3. Identifying existing species
4. No clear policy for government regarding invasive species or bamboo classification(s).
5. Virtually no political will from government to support or develop this business model.

If you think you or your organisation can assist with any of the above, please contact us.
