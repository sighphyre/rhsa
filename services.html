Services


Bamboo Consultants
RHSA offers support to anyone interested in growing, harvesting, designing or building with bamboo.


Bamboo Structures

We offer design and build services for the most popular uses of bamboo, including screens and fences. It is also possible to execute a variety of interior designs as bamboo is strong and comes in many shapes and sizes. We are used to working with a variety of clients including builders, architects and interior designers.


Bamboo Removal / Bamboo Forest Management

This service is contingent on the completion of the Bamboo Regional Hub.


Skills Development Workshops

RHSA offers a skills development workshop consisting of a theoretical and practical component. Over the course of several days, students will learn about the bamboo plant as well as get hands-on with bamboo application in craft and construction. We will keep you posted on this program as it develops, so drop us a line in the meantime.
