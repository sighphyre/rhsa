Services


Bamboo Consultants

RHSA offers support to anyone interested in growing, harvesting, designing or building with bamboo.

Bamboo Structures, Fences and Screens

One of the most popular uses of bamboo is for screens and fences.
It is also possible to execute a variety of interior desings, as bamboo is strong and comes in many shapes and sizes.
We are used to working with a variety of clients including builders, architects and interior designers.

Bamboo Removal / Bamboo Forest Management

This service is contingent on the completion of the Bamboo Regional Hub.

Skills Development Workshops

RHSA offers a skills development workshop consisting of a theoretical and practical component.
Over the course of 3 days, students will learn about the bamboo plant as well as get hands on with bamboo application in craft and construciton.
We will keep you posted on this program as it develops, so drop us a line in the meantime.
